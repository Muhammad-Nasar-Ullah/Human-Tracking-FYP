Why this specific architecture?

Temporal Efficiency

Video processing requires consistent frame-to-frame tracking. YOLOv8's speed allows for identifying humans in every single frame without dropping data, crucial for movement analysis.

Anchor-Free Detection

Unlike previous iterations, v8 eliminates anchor boxes, reducing the number of hyperparameters and improving generalization on diverse human poses found in surveillance footage.

Deployment Ready

Native support for export to ONNX and TensorRT makes it ideal for the final FYP deployment stage, ensuring the visualization runs smoothly on standard hardware.
